Pesto Gnocchi

Ingredients

    li*10
  • 1 pound shelf-stable gnocchi
  • 3/4 cup basil pesto (store-bought or homemade)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes oil drained
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 1/4 cup toasted pine nuts
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan plus more for serving
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the gnocchi according to package instructions. Save 1/2 cup cooking water.

  2. Warm the pesto over medium heat in a large skillet on the stove. Add the reserved cooking water and lemon juice. Give it 2-3 minutes start bubbling and to thicken slightly.

  3. Stir the gnocchi into the sauce.

  4. Fold in the sun-dried tomatoes and spinach. Continue cooking until the spinach wilts, about 1-2 minutes.

  5. Stir in the pine nuts, Parmesan, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper.

  6. Top with additional grated Parmesan as desired before serving.
